MeshTalk

Privacy Policy

Our Commitment: MeshTalk is designed to work entirely offline using mesh networking technology. We believe in your right to private communication, especially during emergencies and in remote areas.

1. Introduction

Welcome to MeshTalk ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how MeshTalk (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information. MeshTalk is an offline mesh communication application that enables messaging without internet connectivity using Bluetooth, Wi-Fi Direct, and Google Nearby Connections.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Display Name: A username you choose during onboarding to identify yourself to nearby peers on the mesh network.
  • Messages: Text messages you send through the mesh network to other users.

2.2 Automatically Generated Information

  • Device Identifier: A randomly generated unique ID (UUID) created locally on your device to identify your device on the mesh network. This is NOT linked to your real identity, phone number, or any personal account.
  • Peer Discovery Data: Basic connection metadata (signal strength, connection type) used to establish mesh network links with nearby devices.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera data, or media files from your device
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data from other apps on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

All data processing happens locally on your device. We use information solely to:

  • Enable peer-to-peer mesh communication between nearby devices
  • Route messages through the mesh network to reach intended recipients
  • Display your chosen display name to nearby mesh peers
  • Store your message history locally on your device for your convenience

Important: MeshTalk operates entirely offline. No data is ever transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers. All communication occurs directly between devices via Bluetooth, Wi-Fi Direct, and Nearby Connections.

4. Data Storage

  • All messages, peer information, and settings are stored locally on your device only using an encrypted local database.
  • No data is stored on external servers or cloud services.
  • You can delete all your data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to advertising networks
  • No data is sent to analytics providers
  • No data is sold to data brokers
  • No data is shared with any third-party services

The only data transmission that occurs is the direct device-to-device communication over the mesh network, which is inherent to the app's core functionality.

6. Mesh Network Communication

When you send a message through MeshTalk:

  • Messages are transmitted directly between devices using Bluetooth, Wi-Fi Direct, or Google Nearby Connections.
  • Messages may be relayed through intermediate devices (multi-hop routing) to reach the intended recipient.
  • Intermediate devices that relay messages do not permanently store the relayed content.
  • Messages include a TTL (Time-To-Live) limit to prevent indefinite propagation.
  • Duplicate message detection prevents unnecessary retransmission.

7. Permissions

MeshTalk requires the following Android permissions to function:

  • Bluetooth: To discover and communicate with nearby devices
  • Wi-Fi: To establish Wi-Fi Direct connections for high-bandwidth transfers
  • Nearby Devices: To use Google Nearby Connections for mesh networking
  • Location (approximate): Required by Android for Bluetooth/Wi-Fi scanning â€” we do NOT track or store your location
  • Notifications: To alert you of new messages and mesh network status
  • Foreground Service: To maintain mesh network connections in the background

8. Children's Privacy

MeshTalk is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we have no servers, we have no means to identify or collect data from any specific age group.

9. Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in security mechanisms
  • The app uses Android's Room database with encryption capabilities
  • No network transmission to external servers eliminates remote data breach risks
  • Mesh communications use the security features provided by the underlying transport protocols (Bluetooth encryption, Wi-Fi Direct security)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible to you at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete all data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Portability: Since data is on your device, you already have full control over it.
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the mesh network at any time by disabling the service in the app settings.
